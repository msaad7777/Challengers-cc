About Us

Challengers Cricket Club (CCC) is a registered Ontario non-profit corporation dedicated to building an inclusive cricket community in London, Ontario. We foster youth development, promote diversity, and bring newcomers, young professionals, and families together through sport. Our members compete in league tournaments across Ontario while creating a welcoming environment for all skill levels and backgrounds. We provide accessible pathways to sport for financially disadvantaged newcomer families, breaking barriers to participation and building community belonging through cricket.
