### Ontario Sport Hosting Program
**Administrator**: Ministry of Tourism, Culture and Sport
**Website**: http://www.grants.gov.on.ca
**Application System**: Transfer Payment Ontario (TPON)

**ðŸŽ¯ MAJOR FUNDING OPPORTUNITY** - This is potentially the **BIGGEST** funding source available to Challengers CC!

**Overview**:
Provincial government program providing substantial funding to organizations hosting national and international sporting events in Ontario.

**Funding Levels**:

**Stream 1: National Events**
- **Up to $100,000** per event
- Sanctioned by national sport organization (Cricket Canada)
- Must have provincial, territorial, or international participation
- Examples: National T20 Championship, Canadian Cricket League Finals

**Stream 2: International Events**
- **Up to $1,000,000** per event
- Sanctioned by international sport organization (ICC)
- Must have athletes from multiple countries
- Examples: ICC Americas Championship, Commonwealth Cricket Tournament

**Eligibility for Challengers CC**:
âœ… **Stream 2 Applicant** - Not-for-profit organizations incorporated in Ontario
âœ… **Registered Ontario NFP Corporation** (#1746974-8) - WE QUALIFY!
âœ… Can apply for both national and international events

**CRITICAL REQUIREMENT**:
âš ï¸ **Must be endorsed by Cricket Ontario** (Provincial Sport Organization)
- This again reinforces the absolute necessity of joining Cricket Ontario
- Endorsement letter required as part of application
- Cricket Ontario must confirm the event aligns with provincial sport development goals

**Application Deadlines for 2025-2026**:

**Intake #1**:
- **Deadline**: January 13, 2025 (11:59 PM EST)
- **Event Period**: April 1, 2025 - March 31, 2026

**Intake #2**:
- **Deadline**: July 14, 2025 (11:59 PM EST)
- **Event Period**: October 1, 2025 - September 30, 2026

**â° URGENT**: The January 13, 2025 deadline is approaching! If Challengers CC wants to host an event in 2025, we need to move quickly.

**Application Requirements**:

**1. Event Proposal & Budget**:
- Detailed event budget (revenues and expenses)
- Government funding cannot exceed **35% of total event budget**
- Must demonstrate other revenue sources (sponsors, ticket sales, etc.)

**2. Endorsement Letters**:
- Letter from **Cricket Ontario** (Provincial Sport Organization)
- Confirms event supports provincial sport development
- Demonstrates event fills gap in Ontario's sport hosting portfolio

**3. Sanctioning Letter**:
- From **Cricket Canada** (for national events)
- From **International Cricket Council - ICC** (for international events)
- Confirms event is officially recognized by governing body

**4. Economic Impact Report**:
- Must use approved models: **TREIM** or **STEAM**
- Demonstrates event's economic benefit to Ontario
- Includes visitor spending, job creation, tax revenue
- Template available through Sport Hosting program

**5. Deficit Guarantee Letter**:
- From organization's board of directors
- Guarantees organization will cover any event deficit
- Shows financial responsibility and planning

**6. Legacy Plan**:
- How the event benefits Ontario sport long-term
- Infrastructure improvements
- Youth development programs
- Increased participation in cricket
- Community engagement outcomes

**7. Accessibility Plan**:
- Ensures event is accessible to all Ontarians
- Venue accessibility features
- Inclusive programming
- Accommodations for athletes and spectators with disabilities

**8. Marketing & Promotion Plan**:
- How the event will be promoted
- Media partnerships
- Social media strategy
- Ticket sales approach
